1. Introduction

Welcome to Elixir Games ("we," "us," or "our"). We develop and publish desktop and mobile games designed to provide engaging, polished gaming experiences. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our games and services.

By downloading, installing, or playing our games, or by using our websites and services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our services.

This policy applies to all games published by Elixir Games across all platforms, including but not limited to:

  • Mobile games on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play)
  • Desktop games on Windows, macOS, and Linux
  • Web-based games accessible through browsers
  • Games distributed through Steam, itch.io, and other digital storefronts

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide when using our games or services:

  • Account Information: Username, email address, and password if you create an account
  • Profile Data: Display name, avatar, and other profile customizations
  • Communications: Messages, feedback, bug reports, and support requests you send to us
  • Purchase Information: Transaction records for in-app purchases (payment details are processed by platform providers)
  • User Content: Custom levels, screenshots, or other content you create and share

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our games, we may automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as IDFA, GAID, or similar), hardware model, and screen resolution
  • Game Data: Gameplay statistics, progress, achievements, scores, session duration, and in-game actions
  • Technical Data: Crash logs, performance data, error reports, and diagnostic information
  • Network Information: IP address, connection type (WiFi/cellular), and general geographic location (country/region level)
  • Usage Data: Features used, buttons clicked, screens viewed, and interaction patterns

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party sources:

  • Platform Providers: Apple, Google, Steam, and other platforms may share limited account information when you connect your accounts
  • Social Networks: If you connect social media accounts, we may receive your public profile information
  • Analytics Partners: Aggregated and anonymized usage data from our analytics providers
  • Advertising Partners: Information about ad interactions and attribution data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

3.1 Providing and Improving Our Games

  • Operate, maintain, and deliver our games and services
  • Save and sync your game progress across devices
  • Process in-app purchases and manage virtual currencies
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Fix bugs, troubleshoot issues, and improve game performance
  • Develop new features, games, and services

3.2 Personalization

  • Customize your gaming experience based on preferences and play style
  • Adjust game difficulty and content recommendations
  • Remember your settings and preferences

3.3 Analytics and Research

  • Understand how players interact with our games
  • Analyze trends, usage patterns, and player behavior
  • Measure the effectiveness of features and updates
  • Conduct A/B testing to optimize user experience

3.4 Advertising

  • Display advertisements within our free-to-play games
  • Measure ad performance and attribution
  • Show relevant ads based on your interests (where permitted)

3.5 Communications

  • Send push notifications about game events, updates, and promotions (with your consent)
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Notify you of important changes to our services or policies

3.6 Safety and Security

  • Detect, prevent, and address cheating, fraud, and abuse
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and community guidelines
  • Protect the rights, property, and safety of our users and company
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party companies that help us operate our business:

  • Cloud hosting and data storage providers
  • Analytics and crash reporting services
  • Customer support platforms
  • Payment processors (for handling transactions)
  • Anti-cheat and fraud prevention services

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

4.2 Advertising Partners

Our free-to-play games may include advertisements from third-party ad networks. These partners may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See Section 5 for more details.

4.3 Platform Providers

When you use our games on platforms like Apple App Store, Google Play, or Steam, those platforms may collect data according to their own privacy policies.

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or government request, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

4.5 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

4.6 With Your Consent

We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

5. Advertising and Analytics

5.1 Third-Party Advertising

Some of our games display advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These partners may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar technologies to collect information about your device and how you interact with ads. This information may be used to:

  • Deliver advertisements
  • Measure ad effectiveness
  • Show ads that may be more relevant to your interests
  • Limit the number of times you see an ad

5.2 Ad Partners We May Work With

Our advertising partners may include (but are not limited to):

  • Unity Ads
  • AdMob (Google)
  • ironSource
  • AppLovin
  • Meta Audience Network

Each partner has their own privacy policy governing their data practices.

5.3 Analytics Services

We use analytics tools to understand how our games are used:

  • Firebase Analytics: For mobile app analytics and crash reporting
  • Unity Analytics: For gameplay data and performance metrics
  • Custom Analytics: Internal tools for game-specific metrics

5.4 Opting Out of Personalized Advertising

You can limit personalized advertising through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Web: Use browser privacy settings or install ad-blocking extensions

Note: Opting out does not mean you won't see ads; you will still see non-personalized advertisements.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Specifically:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period afterward
  • Game Progress: Retained while you use our games; may be deleted after extended inactivity (typically 3+ years)
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely
  • Support Communications: Retained for up to 3 years to improve our services

6.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Access controls limiting who can access personal data
  • Secure cloud infrastructure from reputable providers

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6.3 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Portability

You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. Where technically feasible, we will provide this data in a portable format.

7.2 Correction

You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information.

7.3 Deletion

You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions (such as legal obligations or legitimate business needs).

7.4 Opt-Out Rights

  • Marketing Communications: Unsubscribe from emails using the link provided, or adjust notification settings in-app
  • Push Notifications: Disable through your device settings or in-app preferences
  • Personalized Ads: Use device-level opt-out settings (see Section 5.4)

7.5 Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at privacy@elixirgames.io. Please note that deleting your account will result in permanent loss of game progress.

7.6 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@elixirgames.io. We will respond to your request within 30 days (or as required by applicable law). We may need to verify your identity before processing certain requests.

7.7 Additional Rights for EEA/UK Residents

If you are located in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to restrict processing of your data
  • Right to object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

7.8 California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

We do not sell personal information as defined under CCPA.

8. Children's Privacy

We take children's privacy seriously and comply with applicable laws including the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the United States and similar laws in other jurisdictions.

8.1 Age Requirements

Our games are generally designed for players of all ages unless otherwise specified. However:

  • Features requiring personal information (accounts, social features) are limited to users 13 years of age or older (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction)
  • Some games may have age restrictions based on content ratings

8.2 Information Collection from Children

For users under 13 (or applicable age of consent):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information without verifiable parental consent
  • Games designed for children use limited, privacy-protective data collection
  • We do not serve personalized advertising to known child users
  • We do not enable social features or user-to-user communication for children

8.3 Parental Rights

Parents and guardians can:

  • Review personal information collected from their child
  • Request deletion of their child's personal information
  • Refuse further collection of their child's information

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@elixirgames.io with proof of parental relationship.

8.4 If We Learn of Unauthorized Collection

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without proper consent, we will delete that information as quickly as possible. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.
